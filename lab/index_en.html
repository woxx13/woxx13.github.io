Catalog

Wi-Fi socket

A simple and reliable device that allows you to turn on or off electrical appliances with a capacity of up to 2 kilowatts from your mobile phone, tablet, computer or laptop. This can be a pump in a well, lighting in the yard, garage doors and similar remote objects, which are inconvenient to turn on / off in the usual way for some reason. To work with the device, neither the Internet nor the pre-installation of any applications is needed! Details of use are in the video.

Internet socket

Structurally, this is a complete copy of Wi-Fi socket, but different in software. Those this device also allows you to turn on or off electrical appliances with a capacity of up to 2 kilowatts from your mobile phone, tablet, computer or laptop. But at the same time, physically, you can be even on the other hemisphere of the Earth. However, to implement this functionality of the device, there must be a router with a reliable Internet connection and appropriate settings within its reach.

Automatic tank refill

A device that automatically turns on the pump if the liquid level in the controlled tank has dropped to the minimum level and turns it off if the level has reached the maximum value. The device consists of an original system of reed switches placed in a hermetically sealed polypropylene pipe immersed in a controlled container, a float, a control unit, a power supply and connecting cables.
